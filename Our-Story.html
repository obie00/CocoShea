OUR STORY

from a dream to reality...

Dry, ashy or un-moisturized skin is so frustrating. You know that feeling when a product says 'made with Shea butter' or "natural" and it's almost everything but. Yeah, that feeling is how I felt when I decided to start making my own skin care products. I was tired of dry and ashy skin, especially during the colder seasons, I wanted something natural and moisturizing.

That's the basis of our products- natural and moisturizing. Our products are hand-made from carefully selected natural ingredients. These ingredients are known to relieve many skin problems. Eczema, scars and stretch marks have been reduced with consistent use.

Our products are made with simple ingredients that are familiar to most people. We aim to achieve healthy skin and body the natural way. Every item you order is carefully handmade for you specially.
